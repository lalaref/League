Competition Format

  • Each team is guaranteed at least 7 regular season games.
  • Win = 3 pts  |  Draw = 2 pts  |  Loss = 1 pt  |  Forfeit = 0 pts.
  • Each game consists of 4 quarters, 10 minutes each.
  • Running clock throughout, except during timeouts; clock stops during dead balls in the last 2 minutes of Q4.
  • Ties at the end of regulation in the regular season — no overtime; both teams receive 2 points.
  • Playoff overtime: 2 minutes; if still tied, a golden free throw decides the winner — each team shoots 1 free throw.

Forfeit & Ranking Rules

A team shall be deemed to have forfeited if, within 10 minutes of the scheduled game time, any of the following applies:

  • The team has fewer than 5 players or is unable to take the court;
  • The team fields an unregistered player;
  • One side voluntarily withdraws from the game;
  • One side disrupts the game, making it impossible to proceed (including players, coaches, or spectators).

The forfeiting team will be recorded as having lost 0:20, and will receive 0 points.

If 2 or more teams are tied on points, head-to-head results shall be the first tiebreaker; if still tied, overall goal difference across all group games shall apply.

Player Eligibility & Registration Deadline

  • All players must complete registration and receive league confirmation before representing their team in any game.
  • Each player may only register with one team per season.
  • The deadline for submitting and amending rosters is the date and time specified in the league charter.
  • Players added or changed after the deadline require written approval from the league; otherwise they are ineligible to play.

Rescheduling Policy

  • Rescheduling requests must be submitted at least 72 hours before the scheduled game time, along with a stated reason.
  • A reschedule requires agreement from both teams and the league to take effect.
  • The number of reschedules allowed per team and any additional arrangements are subject to the league's seasonal announcements.
  • Failure to follow the proper rescheduling procedure and absence from a game will be treated as a forfeit.

Adverse Weather Policy

  • If the Hong Kong Observatory issues Tropical Cyclone Warning Signal No. 8 or above, or a Black Rainstorm Warning, games will be suspended or postponed.
  • Whether games proceed as scheduled will be determined by the league's official announcement before or on game day.
  • Makeup dates and arrangements for weather-affected games will be announced by the league as soon as possible.
